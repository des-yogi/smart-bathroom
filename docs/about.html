About us

About our company

We are a proud family-run

We are a proud family-run business with deep connections in the construction industry, now being third generation. Over the last few decades, our family has been participating in a different range of construction and renovation projects - from residential complexes to cultural landmarks. This legacy and dedication to our traditions has shaped who we are today.

Those days our passion and focus shifted to smart, innovative, and sustainable bathroom design and execution. We believe the bathroom should be more than just a functional place - it should be a space of wellness, comfort, and beauty.

That's why we prefer to focus on sustainable, intuitive, organic shapes materials that promote a sense of calm and connection.

Combining modern technology with thoughtful design, we bring a fresh approach to every project- balancing tradition with forward-thinking solutions for a better, smarter way to live.

Our vision is to give an opportunity to everyone for the creation of a well-organized, aesthetically pleasing, and comfortable bathroom that is essential for your long-term wellbeing and health.
